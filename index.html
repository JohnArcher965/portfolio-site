About

Johnathan Wilson

Full-Stack Web Developer & Designer

I have been coding since I was thirteen, ever since we had a computer in the house. I checked out The Complete Idiots Guide To Web Design from our local library and worked through it cover to cover. I was so upset when it was due for return that my parent's bought me a copy, in addition to some other materials. My father was a teacher, so was happy that I was learning something rather than playing games. Over ten years later I still have that book.

I grew up in Rural Lincolnshire so I have always had a passion for the outdoors. Lincolnshire is very flat, so natually I enjoy cycling, however I also like to get away to the Lake District whenever possible for hiking and light mountaineering. I also enjoy some extreme sports such as Skiing and Scuba Diving, and I am a BSAC Qualified Sports Diver. I love skiing in Europe, and frequent the Pyrenees and the Italian Alps. Egypt is my favourite place for Diving, however unfortunately I haven't been able to go for some time. When I'm not working, I'm always up for an Age of Empire's II session.

I enjoy spending time with friends and family, featuring the occasional day trip to Brighton. I can often find myself engrossed in a project so entirely, that I forget to eat, however, when I do come up for food, I would say my favorite is Italian, specifically Carbonara.
